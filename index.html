Stock prediction Model

This research study focuses on developing a predictive model to estimate returns in investment funds. The significance of accurately predicting returns lies in aiding investors’ decision-making processes and optimizing portfolio performance. The study utilizes historical data from a dataset of investment fund performance measures. The main research question aims to understand the factors that influence returns and subsequently build a reliable predictive model. The approach involves exploratory data analysis, time series analysis, and the application of machine learning techniques. The results showcase the development of a linear regression model that predicts returns based on features such as volatility and maximum drawdown. The implications of this research contribute to providing investors with valuable insights for making informed investment decisions.
